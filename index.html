The Studio

The ethics of aesthetics that work.

For over 25+ years, The Right Angle has been an award-winning force in design, shaping transformative environments and products. Our legacy is built on more than a quarter-century of excellence across the integrated disciplines of Interior, Product, Retail, Spatial, and Event design.

This core belief, championed by our dedicated team of design professionals, informs our holistic approach. We orchestrate a seamless journey from concept to reality, managing every detail of the design, bespoke manufacturing, and final installation. This commitment to quality, innovation, and flawless execution has cultivated a discerning clientele, both in India and on the global stage.
